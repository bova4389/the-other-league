## SCORING SETTINGS

**Format:** Non-PPR with TE premium and yardage distance bonuses

**Passing:**
- Pass TD: +4
- Pass Yd: +0.04 (25 yds = 1 pt)
- Pass INT: −2
- Pass 2PT: +2
- Pass TD 40+ yds: +2 bonus
- Bonus 400+ pass yds: +2

**Rushing:**
- Rush TD: +6
- Rush Yd: +0.1 (10 yds = 1 pt)
- Rush 2PT: +2
- Rush 40+ yd play: +1 bonus
- Bonus 200+ rush yds: +2

**Receiving:**
- Reception: 0 (non-PPR)
- Rec TD: +6
- Rec Yd: +0.1 (10 yds = 1 pt)
- Rec 2PT: +2
- TE Premium: +0.5 per reception
- Rec 0–4 yds: +0.5
- Rec 5–9 yds: +0.75
- Rec 10–19 yds: +1.0
- Rec 20–29 yds: +1.0
- Rec 30–39 yds: +1.0
- Rec 40+ yds: +2.0
- Bonus 200+ rec yds: +2

**Penalties:**
- Fumble lost: −2
- INT thrown: −2

**Key strategic implications:**
1. Non-PPR kills volume slot WRs — targets and YAC matter more than catch volume
2. TE premium (+0.5/rec) already makes elite TEs valuable; the 2027 flex upgrade makes them even more so — buy TEs now
3. Super Flex strongly rewards teams with a legitimate QB2
4. Distance bonuses heavily favor downfield threats and big-play backs over checkdown/dump-off players
5. 40+ yard TD bonuses and reception distance tiers reward explosive, deep-threat players
